# Target Audience & Content — Aegis

## Target age group
- 18+ (adults only)
- Not designed for children
- Not a "Made for Kids" app

## Reason
The app handles emergency/safety scenarios. While it protects families including children,
the app itself is installed and configured by adults. Features like remote wipe,
duress PIN, and panic response are adult-managed.

## Appeals to children?
No. No games, no characters, no child-appealing design elements.

## Ads
None. No ads. No ad SDK. No advertising ID collection.

## Teacher Approved program
Not applicable.
