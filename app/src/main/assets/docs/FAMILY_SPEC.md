# Aegis — The Shield

## What is Aegis?

Aegis is an encrypted communication app with a built-in emergency response system. It replaces your everyday messenger with something that also protects you.

Everything is encrypted. Nobody can read your messages, track your location, or listen to your calls — except the people you explicitly authorize.

---

## Why does it exist?

Your phone was stolen during a riot. If Aegis had been installed:

- Your Trusted contacts would have been alerted immediately.
- They would have seen exactly where the thief took the phone on a real-time map.
- The phone would have started recording audio and broadcasting GPS every ten seconds.
- One tap from any paired device: the phone triggers a siren and locks itself.
- The thief gets a locked brick. Your data stays encrypted.

That scenario is not hypothetical. Aegis exists because it happened.

## What does it do?

### Messaging

- Text, photos, voice messages, files.
- Voice and video calls.
- End-to-end encrypted. No phone number. No account. No data collection.
- Disappearing messages, scheduled messages, quiet hours.

### Trust Tiers

Every contact gets a tier that controls what they can see:

| Tier | Access | Who belongs here |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| **Trusted** | Location, status, battery, SOS alerts | Partners, parents, closest circle |
| **Emergency** | SOS alerts only | Doctor, neighbor, trusted colleague |
| **Untrusted** | Messages only | Everyone else |

One tap to set. One tap to change.

### SOS Button

- Hold three seconds on screen, or press power button four times rapidly.
- Sends your location to all Trusted and Emergency contacts instantly.
- Starts recording audio and video.
- Displays a real-time map alert on every paired device.
- Works with the screen locked.
- Screen dims so an attacker believes the phone is off — but it continues transmitting.

### Voyager Mode

When your battery reaches 5%, the phone displays a fake dead-battery screen. It appears completely off. Behind the fake screen, it continues sending your location once per hour — for days.

### Duress PIN

Two PINs for the app. Your real PIN opens Aegis normally. A fake PIN opens a decoy version — clean contacts, plausible message history, no alarms visible. Behind the scenes, it silently triggers SOS and alerts your Trusted contacts.

An attacker forcing you to unlock sees a normal messenger. Your circle sees the alarm.

### Shield Tiers

Your security level is displayed as a colored frame on your avatar. Everyone in your circle can see it.

- **Bronze** — PIN set.
- **Silver** — several security features active.
- **Gold** — all in-app features enabled.
- **Cyan** — Gold plus device-level security hardening.

Higher tier means stronger protection for you and more confidence for your contacts.

### Mugshot

Three incorrect PIN attempts trigger the front camera silently. The photo is sent to your Trusted contacts. The person entering the wrong PIN does not know.

### SIM Swap Alert

If someone removes or replaces the SIM card, your Trusted contacts are notified immediately.

### Canary

A dead man's switch. Set an interval — 24 hours, 48 hours, one week. If you do not open Aegis within that interval, it sends a pre-written message to your contacts indicating something may be wrong.

### Remote Commands

From any paired device, your Trusted contacts can remotely:

- **Locate** — force GPS and return coordinates.
- **Sound a siren** — even if the phone is on silent.
- **Wipe** — factory reset if the phone cannot be recovered.

### Vault

An encrypted safe inside Aegis with its own PIN. Documents, photos, and notes protected behind a second layer of encryption. Even if someone opens Aegis with the real PIN, they cannot access the vault without the vault PIN.

### Geofence

Define zones — school, home, work. Receive alerts when contacts arrive or leave.

### Anonymous Groups

Group chats where members cannot determine each other's identities. Everyone appears as a code name. If you leave, everything is erased from your device — as if the group never existed.

## Location Sharing

Trusted contacts can see each other on a map in real time. GPS runs in the background with minimal battery impact.

Only Trusted contacts can see this. Emergency and Untrusted contacts cannot.

---

## What Aegis does NOT do

- Does not sell your data.
- Does not display advertising.
- Does not allow strangers to contact you.
- Does not require a phone number or email to operate.
- Does not use Google, Apple, Facebook, or any third-party server.
- Does not transmit anything to anyone you have not explicitly authorized.
