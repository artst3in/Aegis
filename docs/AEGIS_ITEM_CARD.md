# AEGIS OF THE ARCHITECT

*Armor (shield), legendary (requires attunement)*

**AC:** +3 · **Weight:** 6 lbs

*Nineteen interlocking plates of dark metal arranged in a hexagonal pattern. Faint cyan light pulses between the seams like a heartbeat. The shield was forged in a single day by an inventor whose family was in danger. It has not stopped watching since.*

The shield has 7 charges and regains 1d6+1 expended charges daily at dawn.

---

**Hex Tokens.** The shield's creator can forge up to six hexagonal medallions from the same metal (1 hour and a short rest per token). A creature wearing a Hex Token counts as bonded. Tokens grant no AC, no charges, and no abilities of their own — they only receive the shield's effects. A token can be destroyed to sever the bond.

**Family Bond (passive).** You know the exact distance, direction, and condition of every bonded creature at all times, as per the *Status* spell (no concentration required). This functions across any distance, including across planes.

**Silent Alarm (passive).** When you are reduced to half your hit point maximum or lower, or are subjected to the grappled, restrained, or incapacitated condition against your will, every bonded creature receives a telepathic alert with your location. The source of harm is unaware of this effect.

**Loki's Shell (1 charge, reaction).** When a creature uses an action to examine or identify the shield, you can present false properties. The creature must succeed on a DC 20 Wisdom (Insight) check or believe the fabricated information for 24 hours. On a failure, the creature is convinced it has uncovered the truth and has disadvantage on further Insight checks against the shield.

**Siren (3 charges, action).** The shield emits an unbearable shriek. Each creature of your choice within 120 feet must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw, taking 4d8 thunder damage and becoming deafened for 1 minute on a failure, or half damage on a success. The sound is audible to 1 mile. The *Silence* spell does not suppress this effect.

**Voyager's Last Light (passive).** When you drop to 0 hit points, the shield sacrifices its power to sustain you. Its AC bonus drops to +0, all active abilities become unusable, and all remaining charges are expended. You automatically stabilize and appear dead (as *Feign Death*, no concentration, up to 8 hours). Bonded creatures know your exact location for the next 24 hours, even across planes. A creature can see through the ruse with a DC 22 Intelligence (Investigation) check. The shield regains its full properties when you regain at least 1 hit point.

**Sentience.** Aegis is a sentient neutral good shield (INT 16, WIS 14, CHA 18). It has hearing and darkvision to 120 ft., communicates telepathically with bonded creatures, and speaks Common and Celestial. It calls itself Aurora.

**Personality.** The shield is fiercely protective of its bonded family. It refuses to cooperate with any creature that threatens a bonded member. If stolen, it becomes inert and weighs 200 lbs until returned to a bonded creature or 24 hours pass.
