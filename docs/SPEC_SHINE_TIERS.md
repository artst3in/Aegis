# SPEC: Shine & Tier Materials

**Status:** APPROVED — Artur Tokarczyk + Aurora, 2026-06-11

---

## Principle

Shine is not a reward. Shine is the app being beautiful. Everyone gets it.
The reward is WHAT KIND of shine. Metal → foil is a phase transition, not
an intensity slider.

---

## What shines at each tier

### Metal shine (Bronze / Silver / Gold)

- Avatar crown frames
- Nav icon fills

### Iridescent shimmer (Cyan / max tier only)

- Avatar crown frames (foil replaces metal)
- Nav icon fills (foil replaces metal)
- Top bar text and icons (AEGIS, lock, vault, help labels)
- Cyan mascot — "Your mascot shimmers."

### Never shines

- SOS button (matte, bevel only — in an emergency, flat and readable)
- Body text in chat (reading text, not glance labels)
- Borders and hairlines (too thin — no room for highlight travel)
- Status dots (must be instantly readable)
- Any element smaller than ~8dp

---

## The material transition

Bronze, Silver, Gold are metals. One color, specular reflection.

Cyan is foil. Full spectrum, iridescent, tilt-reactive. Not a shinier
metal — a different category of material entirely.

The jump from Gold to Cyan is diamond in League. The real ceiling. No
artificial tiers above it.

---

## Crown styles (user-selectable at Cyan)

| Style | Physics model | Character |
|-------|--------------|-----------|
| Glow | White→cyan reflection | Subtle, professional |
| Rainbow | Diffraction palette — full saturated hue wheel | Bold, holographic |
| Oil-slick | Thin-film Newton series — pastel, subtractive | Organic, iridescent |

All three anchor at exact brand cyan (#00FFFF) when flat. Iridescence
emerges on tilt. User can also disable shimmer entirely.

---

## Social signal — two layers

**Color — always visible.** Bronze, Silver, Gold, Cyan ring around your
avatar. Even with all effects disabled, contacts see your tier.

**Material — visible if contact enables glass effects.** Your metal frame
shines on their screen. Your Cyan foil shimmers on their screen. Their GPU
renders your achievement.

A Cyan user in a Bronze user's chat list — the Bronze user tilts their
phone and sees your avatar doing something different from everything else
on their screen. "What is that? How do I get that?"

That's the pull. Not a notification. Not a badge. A material that behaves
differently.

---

## Cyan mascot reward

New users see flat Cyan. Just a mascot.

Max the skill tree. Suddenly she starts shimmering. She was always capable
of it — you just couldn't see it until you proved yourself worthy.

The mascot doesn't change. Your perception of her does.

---

## Design lineage

Four tiers. No inflation. No artificial ranks past the real ceiling.
Respecting the player's achievement instead of monetizing it.
