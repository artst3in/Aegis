## Full description (4000 chars max)

Aegis is an encrypted communication and personal security app. Every message runs through the SimpleX protocol — no accounts, no phone numbers, no metadata, no central server. Nobody can see who talks to whom. Not us, not your carrier, not anyone.

If someone in your circle is in danger, one activation broadcasts their GPS, live audio, and camera feed to every authorized contact. The SOS system works from the lock screen with four rapid power button presses. No app needs to be open.

SECURITY FEATURES

Shield Tier System — a skill tree of ten security features. Enable each to raise your security tier from Bronze to Cyan. Your tier is visible to contacts as a frame around your avatar.

Duress PIN — two PINs for one app. The real PIN opens Aegis normally. The second opens a decoy profile with fake contacts and synthetic messages while silently triggering SOS. The second PIN's existence is cryptographically unprovable.

Encrypted Vault — a separate encrypted storage behind its own PIN. Store sensitive documents, photos, and notes. The vault has its own duress PIN — a third layer.

Mugshot Capture — three incorrect PIN attempts trigger a silent front camera capture sent to your trusted contacts.

SIM Swap Detection — monitors your SIM state. Any carrier or ICCID change triggers an immediate alert.

Canary — a dead man's switch. If you don't open Aegis within a configurable period, a pre-written message is sent to your contacts.

Sentinel Mode — covert intrusion detection. Leave your phone somewhere. It uses ultrasonic sonar, proximity sensors, and the accelerometer as a staged cascade to detect anyone approaching or touching the device. Alerts your contacts silently.

COMMUNICATION

End-to-end encrypted messaging via SimpleX. Voice and video calls via WebRTC. Voice messages, attachments, disappearing messages, scheduled messages, secure notes, group chats, and anonymous group chats where members cannot identify each other.

No accounts. No phone numbers. No servers storing your data. Pair by scanning a QR code. That's it.

DESIGN

LunaGlass design language. Dark theme with cyan accents. As your battery drops, the screen progressively desaturates to gray — but red elements stay vivid, so danger signals always stand out.
